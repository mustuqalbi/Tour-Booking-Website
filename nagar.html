About Hunza and Naga Parbat Base Camp Trek

Hunza and Nanga Parbat Base Camp trek is a specially designed tour package for travelers who wish to explore the beautiful Hunza valley and trek to Nanga Parbat base camp. During the tour, you will be able to get the closest view of Nanga Parbat and Fairy Meadows. Nanga Parbat is the 9th highest 8000 peak in the world with an elevation of 8126m. In addition, you will be able to explore the beautiful Hunza valley till Khunjerab Pass.
